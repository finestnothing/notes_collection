# Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats
Mix all ingredients together, refrigerate for 4+ hours

## Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup milk
	- Can substitute half w/ yogurt
- 1 banana, mashed
- 2-3 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- pinch of salt

## Optional Toppings
- Sliced banana
- Peanut butter
- Chopped nuts
